What is GDSC?

The Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) is a global program for university students interested in Google developer technologies. GDSC Sohar is a local chapter of this program based in Sohar University. It aims to empower students to learn and explore new technologies, collaborate on projects, and solve real-world problems using Google's tools and resources.

About the Challenge

The Oman Uni Tech Challenge 2024 is an initiative aimed at university students to tackle real-world problems aligned with the United Nations' 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). The goal of this challenge is to encourage students to leverage their technical skills and creativity to develop innovative solutions that address pressing global issues such as poverty, education, healthcare, and environmental sustainability. By participating in this challenge, students not only have the opportunity to make a positive impact on society but also gain valuable experience in problem-solving, collaboration, and project management.
